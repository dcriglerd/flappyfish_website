Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Flappy Fish ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when playing our game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Username: If you choose to set a display name for the leaderboard, we store this information.
  • Game Progress: Your high scores, coins, unlocked skins, achievements, and other gameplay data.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Identifier: A unique, anonymous identifier for your device to enable cloud saving and leaderboard features.
  • Gameplay Statistics: Anonymous data about game sessions, scores, and feature usage to improve the game experience.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob
We display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. This may include:
  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Save and sync your game progress across sessions
  • Display your scores on the global leaderboard
  • Track achievements and rewards
  • Provide and improve the game experience
  • Display relevant advertisements

Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored:

  • Locally: On your device for offline play
  • Cloud: On our secure servers for sync and leaderboard features

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Children's Privacy

Flappy Fish is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require any personal information to play. The optional username feature is completely voluntary and should not contain real names or personal information.

Your Choices

  • Advertising: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • Data Deletion: You can delete your local game data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.
  • Username: You can change or remove your username at any time within the game.

Third-Party Links

Our game does not contain links to external websites or third-party services beyond the advertising network.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
