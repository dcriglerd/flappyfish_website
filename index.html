Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Flappy Fish. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Username: If you choose to set a display name for the leaderboard.
  • Game Progress: High scores, coins, unlocked skins, and achievements.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Identifier: Anonymous identifier for cloud saving and leaderboard.
  • Gameplay Statistics: Anonymous data about game sessions.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob
We display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google may collect:
  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

  • Save and sync your game progress
  • Display scores on the global leaderboard
  • Track achievements and rewards
  • Display relevant advertisements

Data Storage and Security

  • Locally: On your device for offline play
  • Cloud: On our secure servers for sync and leaderboard

Children's Privacy

Flappy Fish is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • Advertising: Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • Data Deletion: Delete local data by uninstalling the app.
  • Username: Change or remove your username anytime in the game.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time by posting the new version on this page.
